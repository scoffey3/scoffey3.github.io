The Lakers legend

Mr. Bryant was born in Pennsylvania, but found a new home on the west coast.
Kobe Bryant was a professional basketball player for the Los Angeles Lakers.
He played for the LA Lakers for the entirety of his pro basketball career.
"Mamba" has a resume that speaks volumes about his athletic ability.
Kobe passed away suddenly at the beginning of 2020 in a helicopter accident.
His legacy will live on forever and he will be remembered as one of the greats.
